Evoable Asia – to be no.1 it software provider in asia

Urgent! Junior Android Developer

Ho Chi Minh, District 1 Đăng nhập để xem mức lương

Mô Tả Công Việc

We are in urgent need of enthusiastic and professional developers with at least 1 year experience and strong knowledge in Android to develop applications


* Benefit :

  • Salary package: competitive and negotiable
  • Salary review: 2 times/ year and annual bonus based on performance.
  • Lunch & parking allowance .
  • Premium Health Insurance.
  • Language training: Japanese, English (Company sponsor)
  • Applicable for all normal benefits: insurances, 13th salary, annual outing trip, team building activities, kick off 01 time/quarter.

Yêu Cầu Công Việc

+ Education :Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or equivalent

+ Experience:

  • Good experience and strong skills with Android SDK, Eclipse IDE
  • Experience with web services (JSON, XML, RSS).
  • Knowledge of iOS is a plus.
  • Experience in using some technologies such as GPS, Call, SMS.
  • Experience in using SQL, SQLite database technology

+ Soft skill:

  • Be able to read and understand English documents
  • Be able to speak Japanese is a plus
  • Good sense of teamwork and high responsibility
  • Good communication skill
  • Self-motivated, self-disciplined
  • Able to work both independently and as part of a team

Yêu Cầu Công Việc

Working with us you will work directly with our Japanese experts and absorb the up-to-date technology through a professional training scheme.


Moreover, you will have chances to experience our really cool combination of Japanese and Vietnamese cultures along with our benefits:

  • Events: Quarterly Kick-Off Meeting, Seasonal events, Annual Company Trip
  • Health Care: Annual heath check-up, premium health insurance
  • Training: Japanese/English class, on-job training…
  • Awards & bonus: Most Valuable Person (MVP), 13th month salary, incentive bonus
  • Clubs program: Football, Badminton, Swimming, Tennis, Guitar
  • Others: Lunch supports, EVAteria
